Projects

  • Retro Video Store
    Once upon a time in ye olden days, to watch a movie, a person had to head down to their local video store and rent a video. This app will help an old timey retro video store owner handle the back end of their business. (Completed with Grace (Jiajia) Wang)

  • Task List
    This Task List app allows you to create, read, update, and delete tasks, as well as mark them complete and sort them. When a task is marked as complete, a notification will be sent to Slack. Tasks can also be associated with goals.

  • Solar System
    Store all of the information about planets that you could want! (Completed with Christine Lahens)

  • Swap Meet
    This app allows you to organize a swap meet! You have a bunch of stuff. So do your friends! It would be awesome if each person could swap one of their things with another person's things.

  • Adagrams
    Play Adagrams, a game in which a player is given a random set of letters and must make an anagram using those letters. Each submitted word will score points. (Completed with Sabrina Lauredan)

  • Viewing Party
    You and your friends enjoy watching things together online. Of course, everyone has seen different things, has different favorites, and different things they want to watch. You've been using a spreadsheet to compare everyone's watched list, favorites list, and watchlist, but it's been getting too cumbersome. In order to find things you've watched and your friends haven't watched, or things that your friends have watched and you haven't watched, you have to comb through the spreadsheet. There is now an easier way using this app!
