Well, I won't bore you with too many facts, so I'll start with the basics

My pronouns are she/her.I am currently a student at Ada Developers Academy which is a tuition-free full-stack web development program for women and gender-diverse folks. Previously, I worked as a Sales Development Representative in a Seattle tech company. After some life-changing events and re-evaluation, I discovered the world of programming and decided to pursue it as my career. Was it a difficult decision? Oh, absolutely. Was it the right decision for me? Without a doubt! I wouldn't be able to create my own website otherwise!

In the summer I'll also take walks at the beach or park. And in the winter, I like to have show marathons and go snowboarding at the local mountains here in the Seattle area. My post-Covid and post-Ada goal is to first travel to Japan again, and then to Europe.
