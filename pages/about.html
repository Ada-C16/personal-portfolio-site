Ada vs. Octothorpe

Home Adventure Players
Once upon a time, the demon Octothorpe conquered the great realm Front End.
Queen Ada dared attempt what none had accomplished before: to defeat Octothorpe for good.
100 years have passed since she set out on her quest. No one knows what has become of her.
Will you find Ada and stop Octothorpe, or will Front End be doomed for another 100 years?

Game by Ro Peeples
