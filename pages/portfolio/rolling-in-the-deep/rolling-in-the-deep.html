Rolling in the Deep

7" x 7" | Linocut | May 2021

ABOUT

I think this was one of my earlier big prints. Probably the biggest I had done at that time, a new personal record. It was ... overwhelming, to say the least. But at some point I had gotten in the "zone". The artist zone where my body kind of took over. The kind where I ran a little bit on auto-pilot, let my muscle memory take over. And then I inked it and was so pleasantly surprised. My style was surprisingly compatible with this medium.
