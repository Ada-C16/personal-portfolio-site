About

Hello, I'm Karishma Johnson. I'm a 22 years old lesbian (this is a crucial detail) and am currently enjoying cultivating my skill in printmaking. My work is often presented in sequences meant to build off of one another to convey a greater point. Central to many of my prints are anger, forced recognition, and reclamation of my feelings surrounding mental illness, queerness, and gender expression (reactions to these may vary). I am often inspired to try new things; things that I will find undoubtedly frustrating, confusing, and require an excess in pre-planning (I secretly love this). If you spend a fraction of your existence with something I have created or even deliberately avoid doing so from this point forward, thank you for your participation in our paths momentarily crossing.
